Honeymoon Planning Tips by Claude

Make the most of your October getaway

🌅
October is Prime Season
Jamaica and Aruba sit outside the main hurricane belt. October brings warm temps, low crowds, and off-peak pricing — ideal timing.
💌
Tell Them It's Your Honeymoon
Always notify the resort at booking and again at check-in. Most properties offer complimentary room décor, rose petals, or a welcome amenity for honeymooners.
🛁
Upgrade Your Room Category
Even a one-tier room upgrade — to a suite with a plunge pool or butler service — dramatically elevates the honeymoon experience. Often worth the cost.
🍾
Pre-Book Romantic Experiences
Private dinners on the beach, sunset sailing, couples spa packages, and snorkeling tours sell out. Reserve these before you arrive, not after.
📋
Travel Insurance is a Must
With suites ranging up to $11K+, protect your investment. Look for Cancel for Any Reason (CFAR) policies — especially for October travel in the Caribbean.
✈️
Factor in Flight Costs
Pittsburgh (PIT) has direct options to Montego Bay and connecting routes to Aruba and Costa Rica. Book flights early — October is still competitive for Caribbean routes.
